How to use:

  1. Choose your AI provider (OpenAI or Google Gemini)
  2. Configure your API key and preferences above
  3. Select any text in any application
  4. Polish text: Press your polish shortcut (default: Cmd+Option+P)
  5. Translate text: Press your translate shortcut (default: Cmd+Option+T)
  6. The processed text will be copied to your clipboard
  7. Paste it wherever you need it

Getting API Keys:

  • OpenAI: Get your key at platform.openai.com
  • Google Gemini: Get your key at aistudio.google.com
